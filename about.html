About CramLink Imports

CramLink Imports is a modern importation platform designed to help customers in Botswana purchase products from international online marketplaces easily and securely.

Who We Are

CramLink Imports was created to simplify international shopping for local customers. Many people struggle with overseas payments, shipping, and product ordering. Our platform acts as a bridge between customers and global marketplaces such as Amazon, Alibaba, SHEIN, and Temu.

The system allows users to register accounts, place product orders, track shipments, manage profiles, and monitor their order history through a user-friendly dashboard.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide affordable, reliable, and convenient global shopping solutions for customers in Botswana while improving accessibility to international products.

Our Vision

To become Botswana’s leading online importation assistance platform by offering efficient ordering services, shipment tracking, and excellent customer experience.

Services We Offer

  • International product ordering assistance
  • Shipment tracking services
  • Customer dashboard management
  • Order history management
  • Secure account registration and login
  • Support for local payment methods
